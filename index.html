Who We Are

Ranie Inc. was established 2 years ago. Through the years the company gained a well-established reputation for its commitment to offer quality, timely and unparalleled customer service.
The company has grown and been enriched with a high qualified staff and crew, always aiming at customer satisfaction and the fulfilment of their needs.
Since our clientele is diversified, we offer a wide selection of new models starting from small city cars, medium sized, automatic and luxury cars able to satisfy or give mobile solution at any of your professional or private needs. All car models we provide, are new and in excellent condition.
Due to high quality services, we have built a solid foundation of regular customers and continue to grow, adapt and change as necessary to meet your requirements.
The head office of our car rental company is situated in Monrovia. The locations is centrally located and accessible with public transport.
Our 2 years of experience, our wide selection of cars and our ability to respond quickly to customer needs made us one of the most popular car rental companies in Liberia.
We invite you to enjoy the monuments and the sights here in Libera by travelling with a reliable car of our fleet and don't forget our helpful and experienced staff is always available to help you!
